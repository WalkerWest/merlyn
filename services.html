Three engagement modes at L2 and L3

DIY, Done-for-you, or Collaborative

At L2 and L3, three engagement modes are available. The mode depends on whether your team builds it, Merlyn builds it, or we build it together — and which L1 track your team has been through. Same capability in all three modes; what changes is who owns the operational muscle long-term.

Mode A · DIY

You build it

Your trained team builds and operates after the matching L1 track. Merlyn provides advisory and the platform. Lowest ongoing cost; longest time to first value (training + build).

Requires L1 Data Engineering (for L2) or Agent Construction (for L3)

Mode B · Done-for-you

We build it

Merlyn delivers turnkey. Your team consumes outputs and supervises through TeamIQ. Most regional businesses start here — fastest path to value, predictable pricing.

Requires L1 AI Fundamentals (for users) or Business Leaders (for executives)

Mode C · Collaborative

We build it together

Merlyn architects + your engineers paired daily. Knowledge transfer scoped into engagement. Customers often start Mode C and transition to Mode A after 12-18 months.

Requires L1 Data Eng or Agent Construction (in parallel with build)

Not sure where to start? Most businesses begin at L0 to clear the IT foundation, then add L1 to skill up their team. Most L2 and L3 customers start with Mode B (Done-for-you) and graduate to Mode A or C as their internal capability matures. Our free assessment shows you exactly where you are — which layer to tackle next, and which mode fits.
