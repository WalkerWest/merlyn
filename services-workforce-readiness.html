L1 in context

Where L1 sits in the Merlyn AI OS

L1 is the human layer — the OS users. Without people who can direct AI, the data plumbing in L2 doesn't get used and the agents in L3 don't get supervised. Most customers come to L1 from L0 (the managed IT foundation) or arrive at L1 directly if their IT is already in good shape. After L1, the next step is L2 — connecting your operational data so the AI your team has learned to direct can actually see and act on the work.

← L0 · Managed foundation

The IT foundation that makes L1 work possible — networks, security, help desk.

L2 · Connect data to AI →

API integration, custom ML, and the semantic layer that makes data AI-queryable.

L3 · Deploy agents that act →

Digital twins and operational agents that act on the data L2 has connected.
