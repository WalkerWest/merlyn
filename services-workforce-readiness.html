The curriculum

Five tracks — pick the ones that match your AI OS plan

The track a customer enrolls in depends on which layer of the AI OS they want their team to own. Plan to build L2 in-house? Send your data engineers through the L2-enablement track. Plan to have Merlyn build L3 but want your leadership to direct it well? Send executives through Business Leaders. Tracks combine — a real enterprise rollout usually mixes generalist tracks (everyone) with role-specific deep dives (specific teams).

How tracks are delivered

Three delivery formats — same curriculum, different consumption model

Each track is available in three delivery formats. The format you choose depends on cohort size, scheduling flexibility, and how much customization you want.

Academy cohorts
Multi-company · scheduled quarterly

Standard cohort format. Mixed-company enrollment so participants learn alongside peers from other industries. Scheduled per-quarter calendar. Hands-on labs run on Dominion GPU infrastructure. Best for organizations sending 1–10 people from any single track.

  • Per-seat enrollment
  • Quarterly start dates
  • Mixed-company peer learning
  • Capstone project
Classroom training
Single-company · scheduled when you are

Track content delivered as instructor-led classroom sessions to a single company's team. Half-day or full-day sessions; can be scheduled on your timeline rather than the quarterly cohort cadence. Best for organizations sending 10–25 people from a single team.

  • Half-day or full-day formats
  • On-site, in our facility, or virtual
  • Up to 25 participants per session
  • Customized to your tools
Company workshops
Bespoke topical · built for your context

When a standard track doesn't quite fit, we run a bespoke workshop around your specific situation. Two-day to multi-week formats. Topic-focused rather than role-focused — e.g., "AI in your finance function" or "AI for your operations leadership team during the merger."

  • Custom scope, custom audience
  • Built on your company's actual workflows
  • Often follow-on to an Academy cohort
  • Multi-week engagement format

L1 in context

Trained engineers = L2 and L3 DIY

L1 is the prerequisite for the build-it-yourself option at L2 and L3. After your data engineers go through the L2-enablement track, they can build the L2 layer themselves — Merlyn moves to a light-touch advisory role. After your ML team finishes Agent Construction, they can build the L3 layer themselves. Or if you prefer Merlyn to do the build, your trained team supervises the work effectively. Either way, L1 makes the rest of the AI OS yours.

Build it yourself (post-L1), let Merlyn build it, or work together. Three engagement modes.

Same three engagement modes. Digital twins and operational agents — yours, ours, or together.
