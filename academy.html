What makes it different

Built for practitioners, not slides

Three things set the AI Academy apart from online courses and corporate training programs.

Real GPU hardware

Students train on NVIDIA GPU hardware — the same platform powering production AI across the industry — with AMD Instinct available for open-source and federation infrastructure tracks. No shared cloud instances, no variable availability.

Industry-specific tracks

Five curriculum tracks matched to real industry needs — from infrastructure engineering to digital twin simulation to community node development. You train for your role, not a generic syllabus.

Small cohorts, local delivery

Cohorts of 12–20 students in Evansville, Indiana. Instructor-led by practitioners who use these tools in production. A 4:1 student-to-instructor ratio in the hands-on lab sessions.

Curriculum

Five tracks. One academy.

Each track is a standalone certification pathway. Tracks can be combined — the joint capstone cohort brings infrastructure and simulation together.

Track 1 · 12 weeks

Infrastructure engineering

GPU cluster operations, NVIDIA and AMD hardware, and production AI infrastructure.

  • GPU hardware and cluster setup
  • NVIDIA CUDA, TensorRT, and inference optimization
  • AMD ROCm for open-source federation deployments
  • Networking and storage for AI
  • Monitoring and incident response
  • Power management and cooling
Track 2 · 8 weeks

Enterprise AI bootcamp

Practical AI tools for business teams — the literacy foundation every organization needs.

  • AI tools for your industry
  • Prompt engineering and workflows
  • AI-assisted decision making
  • Data literacy fundamentals
  • Safe and responsible AI use
Track 3 · 12 weeks

Simulation science

Digital twin methodology — build, validate, and run operational simulations for real business decisions.

  • Digital twin foundations
  • Data pipeline instrumentation
  • Simulation model construction
  • Scenario design and validation
  • Julia and Python for simulation
Track 4 · 20 weeks

Vertical practitioner

Deep AI expertise in one of five industry verticals — where domain knowledge meets AI capability.

  • Manufacturing + operations AI
  • Healthcare + clinical AI
  • Insurance + underwriting AI
  • Logistics + routing AI
  • Agriculture + precision farming AI

Node development

Hybrid AI capstone
