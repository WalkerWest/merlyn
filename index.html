Absentee ownership doesn't buy strong businesses. It buys businesses that couldn't see what was coming. AI capability is now the difference between those two kinds of businesses.

The same pattern is playing out in infrastructure. Cable, telephone, internet — private companies built it, extracted rent from captive communities, deferred investment, and sold when the price was right. Data and connectivity are about to be the fifth utility to follow that path. Unless communities build it themselves first.

That's what we're doing at 9700 Highway 57 in Evansville — and what every federation node replicates. Community-owned. Democratically governed. Priced at cost recovery. Structured so it can never be sold.
