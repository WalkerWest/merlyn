Two ways L2 customers use Merlyn

Concrete examples

Process intelligence engagements look different depending on whether the customer already has internal ML capability or needs Merlyn to build and operate the whole stack on their behalf.

Regional hospital · ED admissions forecasting

A regional hospital with no in-house data science team wants to predict daily ED admissions to better staff shifts and reduce boarding times. Merlyn builds a custom predictive model using their EHR data, integrates it with the staff scheduling system, and manages model accuracy as patient mix evolves.

Merlyn answer: L2 ML model deployment

$35–65K build fee · $3,000–4,000/mo · Merlyn retrains monthly · Dominion runs the platform underneath

Regional manufacturer · predictive maintenance workflow

A regional manufacturer wants to predict equipment failures before they happen but doesn't have ML capability in-house. Merlyn builds the predictive maintenance model from their SCADA data, integrates with their CMMS, and surfaces upcoming maintenance recommendations to the plant manager. Monthly optimization as new failure data accumulates.

Merlyn answer: L2 ML model deployment + light agentic integration

$45–75K build fee · $3,500–5,000/mo · Merlyn manages the model lifecycle · Dominion runs the platform underneath

In both cases, the customer brings the business problem and the operational data. Merlyn brings the model development, application engineering, and ongoing application SLA. Dominion provides the platform infrastructure underneath. If the customer eventually builds their own ML team, they can graduate to managing the application directly on Dominion — same platform layer, different application-layer ownership. See the graduation path below.

What it costs

L2 works differently from traditional consulting. Instead of a large one-time project fee, Merlyn charges a smaller build fee — sized to cover development cost — and then a monthly hosting and maintenance subscription. The model stays on Dominion infrastructure, we keep it current, and you keep paying for outcomes rather than artifacts.

Compare: a traditional process consulting firm charges $20K to analyze your processes, then $100K per fix. You own the code, it ages, and you call them again in 18 months. Merlyn charges less upfront and keeps the solution running.

Two SLAs, one relationship. Merlyn's L2 subscription includes the application SLA — your custom models, agents, and workflows running, performing, and integrated correctly. The infrastructure underneath (Kubernetes, GPUs, storage, network, monitoring) runs on Dominion Hyperscale with its own platform-layer SLA. You buy one relationship; the two SLAs stack.
